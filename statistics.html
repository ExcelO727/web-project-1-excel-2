Although Messi has played in 4 professional teams, he is widely known for his career in Barcelona, and the legacy he built at FC Barcelona.

Amount of Goals Messi has scored

Messi has scored 896 goals scored in his career, which is well above average for most players. Many people would consider Messi to be the most efficient scorers in all of football history, considering that he scores a goal about every 105 to 106 minutes, and reaching a peak of one goal every 89 minutes, which is a goal in every single game. This my not seem like a lot, but the average attacking player scores a goal every 300 - 400+ minutes.

This really shows Messi's brilliance when it comes to scoring goals, and his efficiency when it comes to scoring goals. If you include his assists, the goal contribution per minute decreases even more to a goal contribution about every 72 minutes.
