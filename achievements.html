Achievements

Trophies

Messi has won a total of 46 trophies in both team competitions, and individual awards like the Ballon D'or and the Golden Boot. Ballon D'ors are awarded to the best overall player during that season. The Golden Boot is awarded to the player with the most goals that season.

Messi has won numerous trophies both individually and in a team. With Messi winning a total of 8 Ballon D'ors making him the player to win the most Ballon D'ors in their career. He has also won 6 Golden Boots, and while that is not the most that a player has won, it is still much more than most players will ever see in their career.The amount of trophies that Messi has won alone surpasses the amount of trophies that some teams have won in total.

Messi has also won numerous team trophies like the world cup, 2 copa america awards, multiple copa del reys, and champion's leagues trophies.
